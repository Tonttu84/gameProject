Armor fatigue: heavier armor should increase fatigueCost in combat (and possibly MARCHFATIGUE for
marching). Currently all unit fatigue costs are flat — armor type should be a multiplier or addend
on fatigueCost so plate > chain > leather > none. Soldier already has +1 heavy armor penalty as a
start; generalize this across all unit/armor types when adding more variety.

Historical archer mechanics (not yet implemented):
  - Morale suppression from volleys: arrow hits should inflict a temporary morale penalty
    on all units in the target hex, not just kill points. Historically the psychological
    effect of a volley was often larger than the casualties.
  - Forced advance under fire: units taking arrow fire should have a chance to fail a
    discipline check and advance early, breaking formation (Agincourt-style — French knights
    charged through mud because they couldn't endure the arrow storm).
  - Skirmisher behaviour: archers should automatically try to maintain a minimum distance
    from approaching enemies and retreat when melee units close in, baiting charges then
    falling back.
  - Volley bonus for massed fire: multiple archers targeting the same hex should multiply
    the morale suppression effect. A lone archer is irritating; a hundred is terrifying.
